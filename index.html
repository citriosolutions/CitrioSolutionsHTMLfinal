Who We Are

  • You don’t want to disappoint your customer’s because of slowness.

How Does it Works

Unlike other performance testing companies in the market the way of working is different with us.

  • Story Telling: understanding customer needs and familiarize with their process and ecosystem.
  • Data Discovery: Process which data from various sources are gathered, processed and cleaned.
  • Data Analytics: Analyzing the performance data to gain insight from hidden patterns and trends.
  • Load Modelling: Arrive at mathematics for concurrency, throughput and different types of load.
  • Load Test Tools: Identify right set of tools which matches customer needs.
  • Load Test Scenario: Script the identified use cases and scenario creation for all load test types.
  • Result Analysis: Thorough check on SLA and pinpoint the performance bottleneck source.
  • Reporting: Recommendation and detailed report on the load test.
